You seem to be ready to move on to the Pro version of shrib.com.

Here's the quick round of features of shrib.co compared to shrib.com:

  • Have your own private namespace (as opposed to sharing it with everyone on the internet). Having your own namespace means that shrib.co/todo will take you to your private "todo" list. No-one else has access to it without your permission. In the free version, shrib.com/todo will take you to everyone's todo list, and anyone else can access and edit your todo list.
  • Your notes are stored forever (as opposed to being deleted after a while of inactivity).
  • The only exception to being "stored forever": You can delete your notes (as opposed to having your notes linger around the internet)
  • Get unlimited notes in your account (as opposed to the limited number of notes)
  • Access all your notes on any device (as opposed to having your note list only on one device)
  • The Pro version is not only based, developed, and maintained, but even purely run in Switzerland (as opposed to using a global content delivery network and third party services like Google Analytics).
    Switzerland has the strictest privacy laws in the world. All user data is protected by the Swiss Federal Data Protection Act (FADP) and the Swiss Federal Data Protection Ordinance which offers some of the strongest privacy protection in the world for both individuals and entities.
  • Get instant auto-save with every keystroke (as opposed to an auto-save every few seconds)
  • Get direct export of all your data in one .zip file
  • Full-text search through all your notes
  • A professional relationship with Luzi, the creator and maintainer of shrib.co
